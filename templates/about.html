~About This Website~

This is our final project for CS50, which takes the idea of Harvard's Q-guide which allows students to rate a class and a professor, and applies it to Whitney Young.

This project was coded by Nicole Jarmula and Claire Fridkin.

What is the Q Guide?

The Q evaluations provide important student feedback about courses and teachers. The questions are multiple choice, though there's room for comments as well. The more specific a student can be about an observation or opinion, the more helpful their response. {% endblock %}
